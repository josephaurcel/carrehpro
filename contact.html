Frequently Asked

Common Questions
Answered.

How do I apply for a visa with Carreh?

Fill out the contact form above or message us on WhatsApp. We'll assess your situation, confirm eligibility, and guide you through the full application process.

Which nationalities can you assist?

We specialize in assisting Nigerian, African and other non-EU nationals applying to European countries — but we assist clients from all nationalities worldwide.

What documents will I need?

It depends on the visa type, but typically includes a valid passport, bank statements, accommodation proof, travel insurance and a cover letter. Carreh prepares and reviews all documents.

How long does a visa application take?

Processing times vary by country — from 3 days for UAE tourist visas to 2–3 months for digital nomad visas. Carreh keeps you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer concierge services separately from visas?

Yes. Our concierge services (personal shopping, luxury cars, home delivery, bespoke trips) are completely independent of visa services. Contact us for either or both.

Are you based only in Paris?

Our headquarters is in Paris (75011), but we serve clients across Europe, Africa and beyond entirely remotely. Everything can be handled digitally — no need to visit our office.
