About Carreh

Born in Paris.
Built for the World.

Carreh was founded in Paris with a singular vision: to make extraordinary travel and seamless mobility accessible to everyone. Our founder — born in Abeokuta, Nigeria, raised in the world of hospitality, and now based in Paris — understands travel not as a transaction, but as transformation.

From AI-powered itinerary generation to expert visa consulting across 50+ countries, Carreh is the platform that connects Africa and Europe through travel, culture, and opportunity.
